Free Shipping

All orders over ₦80,000 qualify for free standard shipping within Nigeria. This is automatically applied at checkout — no code needed.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you can track it at any time using your order reference number on our Track Order page. You can also find your reference in your confirmation email or under My Account.

International Shipping

We ship worldwide. International orders may be subject to customs duties and import taxes depending on the destination country — these are the buyer's responsibility. Mayree is not liable for customs delays.

For international shipping quotes or inquiries, please WhatsApp us or email hello@mayree.co.
