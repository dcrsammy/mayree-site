Returns & Exchanges

Our Returns Policy

We want you to love your Mayree piece. If for any reason you're not completely satisfied, we accept returns within 14 days of delivery — no questions asked.

Items must be unused, unworn, and returned in their original packaging with all tags attached.

How to Return

1

Contact Us

WhatsApp or email us within 14 days of receiving your order. Include your order reference number and reason for return.

2

We Confirm

We'll confirm your return within 24 hours and send you the return address.

3

Ship It Back

Package the item securely and ship it back. Return shipping costs are the buyer's responsibility unless the item was faulty.

4

Refund Processed

Once we receive and inspect the item, your refund will be processed within 3–5 business days to your original payment method.

Exchanges

Because every Mayree piece is one-of-one, direct exchanges may not always be possible. If you'd like a different piece, we recommend returning your item for a refund and placing a new order.

Non-Returnable Items

Items that have been used, worn, washed, or damaged after delivery are not eligible for return. Sale items marked as final sale cannot be returned.

Faulty Items

If your item arrives damaged or faulty, please contact us immediately with photos. We will arrange a full refund or replacement at no cost to you.
