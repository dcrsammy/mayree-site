14-Day Returns

If you're not completely happy with your purchase, we accept returns within 14 days of delivery. No complicated forms. No stress.

What We Accept

✓ Items returned within 14 days of delivery
✓ Items in original, unused condition
✓ Items in original packaging with all tags attached
✕ Items that have been used, worn, or show signs of wear
✕ Items returned after 14 days from delivery date

How to Return

1
Contact Us
Email hello@mayree.co or WhatsApp us with your order reference and reason for return.
2
We Confirm
We'll confirm your return within 24 hours and provide return instructions.
3
Ship It Back
Send the item back in its original packaging. Return shipping costs are covered by the customer.
4
Refund Processed
Once received and inspected, your refund is processed within 3–5 business days to your original payment method.
